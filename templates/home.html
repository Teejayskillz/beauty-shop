Our Story of Care

At Veloura Beauty, we believe every woman deserves to wake up with hair that's protected, hydrated, and beautiful. Our bonnets are more than sleepwear — they're a commitment to your crown.

Founded by hair enthusiasts who were tired of waking up to dry, tangled strands, we set out to create the most luxurious yet accessible protective bonnets on the market.

Each piece is crafted with premium fabrics sourced for softness, durability, and hair-health benefits. Experience the Veloura difference — where beauty meets care in every stitch.
